Graph Drawing Conference

Graph drawing is different from graph editing in that graph drawing is mainly concerned with the persentation of complex connected graphs according to certain aestic rules: for example, avoid edge crossings. Graph editing, on the other hand, focuses on interactive manipulation of graphs by the user.

  • 1998 in Montreal, Canada
  • 1997 in Rome, Italy
  • 1996 in Berkeley, California
  • 1995 in Passau, Germany
  • 1994 in Princeton, New Jersey
  • 1993 in Paris, France
