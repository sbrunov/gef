GEF Users Survey

Thanks for using or evaluating GEF. GEF is a "community software development effort" in that many of GEF's 500+ users have contributed comments, suggestions, project ideas, and code. This survey is intended to gather data to help improve and focus efforts for GEF's code, web site, and documentation.

If you check the box labeled "Don't quote me", then your information will be used only for the purpose of improving GEF. If this box is not checked, then I may redistribute any information you provide for one of the following purposes only:

  • User testimonials on the GEF web page
  • GEF Project ideas
  • List of GEF contributors
  • Items in tech transfer reports to research funding agencies
Please fill in all appropriate fields is possible, but feel free to leave some items blank if you do not want to provide that information. You should be able to fill out the survey in less than five minutes. Thanks for your contribution to the future of GEF. jason!
