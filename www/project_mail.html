Project GEF Mailing Lists

Previously, the GEF project hosted its mailing lists at another service provider, eGroups.com. Interested developers now have a choice as to where to subscribe and where to post.

To subscribe or unsubscribe, send an email message to the email address associated with each "subscribe" or "unsubscribe" line below. The subject line and message body are not used, so you may leave them blank.

announce@gef: Announcements of new releases and other major project events.
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: announce-subscribe at gef.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: announce-unsubscribe at gef.tigris.org
 
dev@gef: The main list for developers to discuss development issues, and general discussion of diagram editing as relevant to GEF.
Archive: view on-line
Post Message: dev at gef.tigris.org
Subscribe: dev-subscribe at gef.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: dev-unsubscribe at gef.tigris.org
 
bugs@gef: Automated notifications of bugs reported against gef itself.
Archive: view on-line
Post Message: bugs at gef.tigris.org
Subscribe: bugs-subscribe at gef.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: bugs-unsubscribe at gef.tigris.org
 
cvs@gef: Automated notifications of source code changes.
Archive: view on-line
Post Message: cvs at gef.tigris.org
Subscribe: cvs-subscribe at gef.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: cvs-unsubscribe at gef.tigris.org
